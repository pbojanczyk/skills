# Change Management Plan Generator

Generate structured change management plans for AI adoption, digital transformation, and technology rollouts. Built by [AfrexAI](https://afrexai.com).

## Why This Exists

70% of organizational change initiatives fail — not because the technology doesn't work, but because people aren't brought along. This skill gives you a complete playbook so your rollout doesn't become a statistic.

## What You Get

A tailored change management plan covering:

- **Stakeholder Analysis** — Map every affected group using ADKAR, identify champions and blockers
- **Communication Plan** — Pre-launch, launch, and post-launch messaging with owners and timing
- **Training & Enablement** — Tiered training (awareness → competency → expertise) with materials list
- **Resistance Mitigation** — Pattern recognition and response strategies for common pushback
- **Phased Rollout** — Pilot → Early Adopters → Majority → Laggards with go/no-go gates
- **Success Metrics** — Leading and lagging indicators with tracking cadence
- **Risk Register** — Probability, impact, mitigation, and trigger conditions
- **Timeline & Budget** — Week-by-week milestones and cost estimates

## Use Cases

- Deploying AI agents across business operations
- Migrating from legacy systems to modern platforms
- Introducing new SaaS tools company-wide
- Rolling out compliance or security frameworks
- Restructuring teams around automation

## Quick Start

Tell your AI agent:
```
Generate a change management plan for deploying AI agents
across our customer support team (45 people). We're a B2B
SaaS company. Target go-live is 8 weeks from now. VP of CX
is sponsoring. Main concern: agents think they're being replaced.
```
